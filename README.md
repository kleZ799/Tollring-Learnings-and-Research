## Overview

Welcome to my AI Engineering Lab.

This repository is a continuously evolving collection of research, technical notes, experiments, architecture studies, and implementation learnings from my journey into Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Models, Databases, and Production AI Systems.

It started during my internship as an AI & Database Intern and has since grown into my personal engineering knowledge base.

The objective isn't to memorize technologies.

The objective is to understand **why they exist, how they work internally, where they fail, and how they scale.**

- What are the trade-offs?
